Game Description

COXETA - Duel-Laned Music Game invites players into a dynamic and mind-bending rhythm experience like no other. Blending high-tempo beats with visually stunning environments, this game lets you tap, slide, and hold through tracks that defy space and time....

Collaborate with the iconic O2JAM universe as you explore the role of a researcher at the Extraordinary Science & Technology Institute, uncovering musical anomalies across dimensional rifts. Each level is a performance and a discovery, challenging you to sync with the rhythm and lead the charge in the Rhythm Revolution.
