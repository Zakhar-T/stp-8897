How to Play

  1. 1

    Choose a Level

    A rhythm game with a story? I'm hooked! The dual-lane gameplay is so fresh.

  2. 2

    Follow the Rhythm

    Tap, slide, and hold to match the notes on two tracks.

  3. 3

    Unlock Content

    Earn points to unlock songs, visuals, and customization.

  4. 4

    Progress the Story

    Complete musical missions and uncover dimensional secrets.

  5. 5

    Rank Up

    Improve your accuracy and timing to climb the leaderboard.

  6. 6

    Join Events

    Compete in time-limited music battles and challenges.
